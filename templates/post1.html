How to gain weight and muscle as a skinny teen? The ultimate guide to bulking up (no BS!)

Aug 22th 2023 5 Min read

Look - I get it, you're eating a lot, probably even more than your non-skinny friends, and you still have not gained any weight or muscle in the slightest. This happened to me, too. I was always a super skinny kid. In 6th grade I weighed 50 pounds (22 kg). In my freshman year of high school, I was an 88 lb (40 kg), 5'5 male. In 2 years time, I gained 70 pounds (158 lb / 72 kg) and heavily upgraded my physique.

Here's how I did it.

1. Are you really eating enough?

If you think you are eating enough, you are probably not. This was probably the most important thing I did to gain weight.

Fueling your body with the right nutrients is the cornerstone of your weight gain and muscle-building journey. The key is to consume a calorie surplus, which means eating more calories than your body burns. Yes, it's time to eat, eat, and eat some more! Unlike those lucky individuals who seem to gain weight effortlessly, you may find that you have a faster metabolism, requiring you to consume more calories than your peers. Embrace this opportunity to explore a world of culinary delights and savor your meals like never before. The biggest tip I have for you is to track your calories. This will help to ensure you are eating enough.

One helpful tool for this is the "MyNetDiary" app. It's totally free and I am not related to it in any way. It is just one of the most helpful tools I used to track my calories. It has a wide variety of foods (pretty much everything) and you just click on the food and your serving size. I know it may seem a little tedious but this app makes it real quick, trust me. This app helped me a lot to track my daily food intake, and it will help you stay on top of your macronutrient balance and overall calorie consumption.

Focus on consuming a well-rounded diet that includes lean proteins, complex carbohydrates, and healthy fats. Incorporate foods such as chicken, turkey, fish, eggs, beans, whole grains, fruits, vegetables, nuts, and seeds into your meals. Aim for a balance of macronutrients in each meal to support muscle growth and overall health.

In addition to calorie surplus, prioritize protein intake to support muscle repair and growth. Include protein sources like lean meats, dairy products, legumes, and plant-based proteins in your diet. Spread your protein intake throughout the day to ensure your muscles have a steady supply of amino acids.

Don't neglect the importance of hydration. Drinking enough water is crucial for overall health and proper muscle function. Aim to drink at least eight glasses of water per day, or more if you engage in intense physical activity or live in a hot climate.

2. Are you working out correctly?

Now that we have the nutrition aspect covered, it's time to hit the gym (or home gym - equally as good) and unleash your potential for muscle growth. A well-designed workout routine will be your secret weapon to gaining muscle mass and strength.

Weightlifting will be the primary focus of your gym sessions. This form of exercise helps stimulate muscle growth by putting stress on your muscles and triggering the body's adaptive response. But fear not, we're not suggesting you need to become a bodybuilder overnight! Instead, we'll guide you through a balanced and effective workout routine that targets all major muscle groups.

If you're new to the gym, let's start with compound exercises—multi-joint movements that engage multiple muscles simultaneously—such as squats, deadlifts, bench presses, and overhead presses. These exercises are the foundation of any muscle-building program, as they recruit the most muscle fibers and provide the greatest potential for growth. Incorporating these movements into your workouts will help you build a solid foundation of strength and muscle.

I encourage you to research and curate a specific workout plan by yourself. No plan that I put here will work for everyone, you should spend a little bit of time curating one yourself. Start by searching up "best workout plan to gain muscle" on Google, you'll find many and be introduced to the wide amount of plans there are out there.

Oh yeah, if you plan on doing workouts at home, here's another free tool that will definitely help you and has helped me with my past home workouts. It's called "Tysofit". It's a personal trainer app that guides you on how your form should be on whatever exercise it is you're doing, and automatically counts the reps of the exercise. They have a free desktop version right now, if you want you can put your computer in front of you while you're working out and test it out, it's pretty cool. Here's the link to it. Tysofit

As a skinny teen, you may be concerned about overtraining or not seeing immediate results, don't. This is unconventional advice, but this has worked for me and a lot of other people who I coached. Hit high-rep, high intensity workouts, and get good sleep. It's important to understand that muscle growth takes time and consistency. Aim to hit the gym regularly, ideally four to five times a week, and gradually increase the weights you lift over time. This progressive overload principle will ensure that your muscles are continually challenged and stimulated for growth.

Consistency and perseverance are the keys to success. It's not about lifting the heaviest weights right from the start; it's about progressive improvement. Track your progress, set achievable goals, and push yourself to overcome plateaus. With dedication and determination, you'll witness significant gains in both strength and muscle definition.

3. Mindset and Lifestyle Factors

Achieving your weight gain and muscle-building goals extends beyond the kitchen and the gym. Adopting a positive mindset and implementing healthy lifestyle habits will greatly enhance your results and overall well-being.

Patience and persistence are essential on this journey. As a skinny teen, you may find yourself comparing your progress to others or getting frustrated when results aren't immediate. It's important to remember that everyone's body is unique, and progress will vary. Stay focused on your own journey, celebrate every milestone, and keep moving forward. Rome wasn't built in a day, and your dream physique won't magically appear overnight.

Rest and recovery are crucial components of muscle growth. Aim to get sufficient quality sleep each night and ensure you're properly hydrated throughout the day. These factors contribute to your body's ability to repair and rebuild muscle tissue, supporting your overall progress. Additionally, allow yourself adequate rest days between workouts to prevent overtraining and promote optimal muscle recovery.

Incorporate healthy lifestyle habits into your daily routine. Stress management, self-care, and a supportive social circle play vital roles in your overall well-being and motivation. Surround yourself with friends and family who support your goals and provide encouragement along the way. By fostering a positive mindset and practicing self-care, you'll create an environment conducive to achieving your weight gain and muscle-building aspirations.

Congratulations, teens, on taking the first steps toward transforming your physique and gaining the muscle you desire! This comprehensive guide has equipped you with the knowledge and tools necessary to embark on your weight gain and muscle-building journey.

Remember, building muscle and gaining weight is a process that requires patience, dedication, and consistency. By embracing the power of nutrition, using apps like Tysofit and MyNetDiary, hitting the gym with a purpose, and adopting a positive mindset, you're setting yourself up for success.

Stay committed, stay motivated, and believe in yourself. It won't always be easy, and there may be moments of doubt, but remember that every effort you put in brings you closer to your goals. Embrace the challenge, celebrate your progress along the way, and enjoy the transformation that will unfold. Listen to your body, make adjustments as needed, and prioritize your safety and well-being throughout your journey.

You've got this, skinny teens! It's time to unlock your full potential, gain weight, and build the muscle you desire. Embrace the process, embrace the hard work, and embrace the incredible transformation that awaits you. Let's make gains, have fun, and become the best version of ourselves together!
