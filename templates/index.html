    • Real-time Rep Counting and Form Correction with Advanced AI Guidance

    Tysofit uses advanced AI technology to count your reps and check your form in real-time, providing guidance and form correction to help you achieve your fitness goals.

    Track your progress over time with comments and pictures to stay motivated

    Tysofit allows you to track your progress over time, with the ability to add comments and pictures to each workout. This makes it easy to see how far you've come and stay motivated.

    Intuitive user interface designed to make you feel motivated

    Tysofit's user interface is thoughtfully curated with intuitive designs and clear instructions, making it accessible and user-friendly for individuals of all ages and fitness levels. The visually appealing design, coupled with interactive features, elevates your motivation levels during workouts.

    Includes a wide range of bodyweight and gym exercises, catering to all fitness levels

    Tysofit includes a wide range of bodyweight and gym exercises, catering to all fitness levels. This allows you to find exercises that are suitable for your current fitness level and progress as you get stronger.

    Coming soon: progress analysis and insights

    Tysofit plans to provide advanced analysis and insights into your progress, helping you understand your strengths and weaknesses and make informed decisions about your training.

    Coming soon: compatibility with apple watch

    Tysofit plans to allow users to sync with your Apple Watch, allowing for added convenience and seamless tracking capabilities. This makes it easy to keep track of your progress and stay on top of your fitness journey.

    Coming soon: meditation and mindfulness games

    Mental health is just as important as physical health, which is why we plan to launch a new feature called Tysofit Rocket. Rocket is a meditation game that encourages and tracks your meditation practice. In this game, the rocket ascends as you engage in meditation, and the longer you meditate, the higher the rocket goes. By incentivizing the meditation process, Tysofit Rocket aims to enhance your mindfulness practice and provide a fun and interactive way to track your progress.

    About

    Tysofit is a cutting-edge app that helps users achieve their fitness goals. With advanced AI technology, Tysofit counts reps, checks form, and provides real-time guidance and form correction. The app tracks progress over time with comments and pictures and offers advanced analysis and insights into the user's progress. With customizable workout plans and exercises tailored to each user's specific needs and goals, Tysofit is the ultimate workout companion.
